Thai Curry

  • 500g chicken breast (filet)
  • 2 Capsicum
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 200g sugar snap pea
  • 2 spoons of sesame oil
  • 2-3 spoons of red curry paste
  • 800ml coconut milk

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken, capsicum, spring onions, carrot and sugar snap peas
  2. Heat up oil in a wok, add red curry paste and coconut milk until it starts boiling
  3. Add chicken and let it cook for five minutes. Then add the remaining ingredients and let it cook for another five minutes
